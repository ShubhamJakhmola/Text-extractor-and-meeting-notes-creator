## 📍 INSTALLATION - READ THIS FIRST!

### **⚠️ IMPORTANT: File Placement**
1. ✅ Place **AutoNote.exe shortcut** on your **Desktop**
2. ✅ Keep **parent folder** in **C:\AutoNote** or any safe, permanent location
3. ⚠️ **DO NOT** delete or move the parent folder after creating the shortcut

---

## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

### **Step 1: Launch**
Double-click **AutoNote** shortcut on your desktop

### **Step 2: Select Images**
- Click **"📁 Select Images"**
- Choose meeting photos/screenshots (whiteboard, notes, etc.)

### **Step 3: Generate**
- Click **"🚀 Generate Smart Notes"**
- Wait 10-30 seconds for AI magic ✨

### **Step 4: Save**
- Click **"💾 Save Notes"** to export

## 🔧 Quick Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Tesseract Missing" | Check `tesseract_engine` folder exists |
| "No Text Extracted" | Use clearer, higher-quality images |
| "API Error" | Check internet connection & retry |
| Poor quality notes | Ensure images are readable & well-lit |
